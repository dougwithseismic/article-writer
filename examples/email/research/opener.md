## Trend
The week's releases cluster around a theme: AI tools are moving from "assistant" to "autonomous agent." Qodo 2.0 uses multi-agent architecture. Ollama is bridging local models to agentic coding tools. The shift is from autocomplete to systems that reason about your codebase.
